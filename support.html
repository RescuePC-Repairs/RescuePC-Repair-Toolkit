Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install RescuePC Repairs?

Download the installer from our website and run it. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation process. Make sure you have administrator privileges on your Windows system. The installation typically takes less than 5 minutes.

What are the system requirements?

RescuePC Repairs requires Windows 10 or later, 4GB RAM minimum, 1GB free disk space, and an active internet connection. For optimal performance, we recommend 8GB RAM and 2GB free disk space.

How do I get technical support?

You can reach our technical support team through multiple channels: email support, our knowledge base, or by joining our Discord community. We provide 24/7 support to ensure you get help whenever you need it.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. All transactions are secure and encrypted. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our product.

Is my data safe with RescuePC Repairs?

Yes, absolutely. We take data security seriously. RescuePC Repairs uses industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We never collect or store sensitive personal information.

How often is the software updated?

We release regular updates to improve performance, add new features, and enhance security. Updates are automatic and free for all licensed users. You'll always have access to the latest version of RescuePC Repairs.

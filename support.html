Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install RescuePC Repairs?

Download the installer from our website and run it. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation process. Make sure you have administrator privileges on your Windows system.

What are the system requirements?

RescuePC Repairs requires Windows 10 or later, 4GB RAM minimum, and 500MB of free disk space. A stable internet connection is recommended for updates and online features.

About RescuePC Repairs

Our Mission

RescuePC Repairs was founded with a simple mission: to provide professional-grade Windows PC repair tools that anyone can use. We believe that computer repair shouldn't require expensive technicians or complex technical knowledge.

Who We Are

RescuePC Repairs is a software company specializing in Windows PC repair and maintenance tools. Founded by Tyler Keesee, a Computer Science student and self-taught high-end gaming PC builder with 6+ years of experience, we've developed a comprehensive toolkit that can fix virtually any Windows PC issue.

Our Story

What started as a personal project became RescuePC Repairs. As a Computer Science student who taught himself to build high-end gaming PCs at age 18, Tyler spent countless hours fixing his own and clients' computers. The manual, time-consuming process of repairing PCs one by one inspired him to create a better solution.

After 6+ years of building and repairing high-end gaming PCs, Tyler knew there had to be a more efficient way. Instead of sitting there doing everything manually, he created RescuePC Repairs to automate the entire process. What began as a personal project to save time became a professional-grade toolkit that revolutionizes PC repair.

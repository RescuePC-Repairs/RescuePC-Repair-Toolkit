GDPR Compliance

Last Updated: June 8, 2025

At RescuePC Repairs, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This GDPR Compliance page outlines our commitment to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and how we handle your personal information.

Your Data Protection Rights Under GDPR

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access - You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification - You have the right to request correction of any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • Right to Restrict Processing - You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability - You have the right to request that we transfer the data we have collected to another organization, or directly to you.
  • Right to Object - You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal personal data, which is only required for payment processing through our secure payment provider, Stripe:

  • Contact Information (name, email address) - Required for payment processing and license delivery
  • Payment Information - Processed securely through Stripe, we never store your payment details

Important Note: RescuePC Repairs is an offline tool that runs locally on your computer. We do not collect any personal data from your computer or system. The only data we collect is what is necessary for processing your payment and delivering your license.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data solely for the following purposes:

  • To process your payment securely through Stripe
  • To deliver your license key via email
  • To provide customer support when requested

Since RescuePC Repairs is an offline tool that runs locally on your computer, we do not collect, store, or process any personal data from your system. The only personal data we receive is through Stripe during the payment process, which is minimal and used solely for license delivery.
